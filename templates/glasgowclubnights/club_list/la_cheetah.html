LaCheetah Night Club

> Named after a famous New York salsa club, La Cheetah Club is situated in the basement of Max's Bar on Queen Street in Glasgows' City Centre.

> The club consists of an isolated DJ booth, Funktion One soundsystem, lighting desk, small cloak room and bar.

> The aim of club space is to offer the opportunity to see some of the most exciting names in electronic music along side local talent in an especially intimate environment, with great sound and an inclusive atmosphere.
